Medicines Information: eMIMS contains prescribing information on the vast majority of medicines available in South Africa – both the abbreviated MIMS entries and the fully detailed MDR listings feature.

Medicines information is searchable by:

  • Ingredients
  • Products
  • Pharmacological Classification
  • Manufacturers

ICD10 Browser: eMIMS contains a fully up-to-date ICD10 browser, with an expandable and a collapsible list structure which makes finding codes and their relevant therapeutic sections quick and easy.
